About

Meet Nathan

Your Guide to Charleston's Most Coveted Neighborhoods

I'm Nathan Overbey, a real estate advisor and Mount Pleasant specialist with over 12 years of experience in Charleston's most sought-after residential communities. My career in real estate began with a simple belief: that the right home isn't just about square footage and price—it's about neighborhood fit, lifestyle alignment, and long-term value.

That philosophy shaped my decision to specialize exclusively in Old Village, Old Mount Pleasant, and the I'On neighborhoods. These aren't just where I work; they're communities I know intimately. I've watched them evolve, helped families build roots in them, and earned the trust of hundreds of clients by delivering the kind of hyperlocal expertise that larger teams simply can't match. I know which oak trees line which streets, how neighborhoods drain during heavy rain, which historic homes require specific restoration expertise, and which opportunities never reach the public market.

I'm particularly passionate about serving out-of-state relocators navigating Charleston for the first time. Moving to a new region can feel overwhelming, but when you have an advisor who knows every corner of your target neighborhoods—and who can speak candidly about what makes each one unique—the process becomes far less daunting. I serve as both real estate guide and relocation advisor, helping clients understand not just the home they're buying, but the lifestyle and community they're joining.

I'm a Five Star Agent and Top Producer, recognizing my commitment to excellence and client satisfaction. I'm fluent in English and Spanish, and I'm a proud member of The Cassina Group, where I collaborate with a team of trusted professionals who share my standards for integrity and service.
